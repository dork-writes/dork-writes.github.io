The Splendiferous Dusk

I like how dusk establishes a tranquil aura everywhere. Soft, continuous hues illuminate the sky, pouring awe in every eye; what a nonpareil ambiance! The sun flickers in clouds, spraying orange and yellow colors across the blue-black canvas of the sky, reluctantly trundling towards west. The waves all settle down, the tides all shush, genuflecting in reverence. The trees freeze, absorbing the captivating atmosphere. And the birds, chirping harmoniously, praise the stupendous scene while heading home. The landscape leaves me dumbstruck. Well, why wouldn't it? After all, it's one of God's most prized paintings: The Splendiferous Dusk...
